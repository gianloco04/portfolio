ABOUT
ME

Hi! My name is Gianluca.

I'm 20 years old and currently studying and working part-time. In my free time, I enjoy improving my Graphic Design and Coding skills by working on personal projects. The area of business I’m most passionate about is Branding and User Experience, as it’s where both my creative and analytical skills come together best.

I live in Mallorca and speak four languages fluently: Spanish, Cathalan, English and Italian.

Thank you for your time, and I hope you enjoy my website. If you like it and want me to help with yours, please get in touch!

STUDIES
EXPERIENCE

I'm currently studying a Bachelor's Degree in Business and Technology at UDIMA, which includes subjects focused on business management (Accounting, Taxation, Data Analysis...) as well as others centered on the digital world (Programming, Service Design, Disruptive Technologies...).
